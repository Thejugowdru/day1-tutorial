Maharaja Institute of Technology Mysore was established in the year 2007 by MET® in a serene location surrounded by sprawling landscape a bit offset from the urbane disruption. Institute is initiated and built by a group of reputable people recognized for their eminence in the field of Science and Technology. Institute was born as a casting of its founders eminence and rich experience poured into the mould of their values and aspirations. The Institute made its beginning with an intake of 300 seats encompassing five engineering disciplines. As a toddler in technical education, the institute shown a brisk growth to its potential leaving foot marks at numerous miles stones soon just in a decade. Institute owns a stature known for serving the society with quality education and is well accepted by all the stakeholders. To the date, the institute is nurturing an intake of 900 plus offering various programs at undergraduate, postgraduate and research level. The alumnae of this institute have been extensively acknowledged by the society and industry alike.

The Institute is renowned as ‘Teaching Centric’ being resourced with knowledgeable and committed faculties showing acute keenness for teaching. The Trust members have made it possible to instigate excellence in technical education through qualitative teaching and learning process. MITM evaluates the success of its programs using the highest standards of quality, innovation and transparency to educate the students to become leaders in their areas of interest. In this regard, the institute is equipped with all modern learning aids to make teaching-learning process a pleasure for all its stakeholders. Along with modern day infrastructure, institute has its over 3000 students commuting through its well planned transportation facility running 50 plus buses with a wide spread network 60km in radius.
